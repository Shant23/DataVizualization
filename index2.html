Examination of Education, Healthcare and Military Spending Accross the G20

This website examines the latest available data on Healthcare, Military and Education spending accross the the worlds richest 20 countries known as the G20.

Many international geopolitical and internal national changes over the past few years have led to a differing expenditure in the three different areas examined. To better understand some of these variables, we will first examine the total population, and gross domestic product or GDP as well as the total national expenditure as a percentage of their GDP.

Population of the G20 in 2017

China, India, Indonesia and Turkey have achieved some of the biggest GDP growth rates, and they currently are considered the biggest emerging markets with strong economies. The remaining members have had large variations, mainly due to the current economic situation and tehir nature as emerging economies.
